What this book is about

Offshore teams fail for predictable reasons — and that is precisely why they can be fixed.

Most companies set up offshore teams to cut costs and end up with exactly what they paid for. The Pivot Model reframes the conversation entirely, drawing on 25+ years building world-class product engineering across India, the US and Europe.
