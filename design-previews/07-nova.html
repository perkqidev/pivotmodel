How it works

From firefighting to a predictable, high-maturity team.

Offshore teams fail for predictable reasons. The Pivot Model gives you the vocabulary, structure and tools to fix them — built on 25+ years across India, the US and Europe.

Benchmark your team across four pivots.

Score every capability against clear L1–L3 criteria with evidence. The Engineering Maturity Benchmark turns gut-feel into a measurable picture.
