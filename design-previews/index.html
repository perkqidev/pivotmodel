The Pivot Model · Phase 2 · Premium + 3D

Design directions

Two tiers, nine directions. The cutting-edge tier uses real WebGL / Three.js 3D, GSAP scroll animation and smooth scroll. The premium tier is art-directed editorial with photography and cinematic motion. Pick one — I build the complete site in it.

Open with internet + a modern browser (Chrome/Edge). The 3D pages load Three.js, GSAP & Lenis from CDN — give them a second, then move your cursor and scroll. If WebGL is unavailable they fall back gracefully. Imagery is placeholder; the real build uses commissioned photography.
